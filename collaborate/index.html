BandLab Invite Link Copier

Step 1: Add the Bookmarklet

Drag the red button below directly to your browser's bookmarks bar. This will create the bookmarklet.

Copy BandLab Invite Link

Step 2: Open Your Bookmark Bar

If you dont see your bookmarks bar:

  • Most browsers/Chromium Based Browsers: Press Ctrl + Shift + B (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + B (Mac) This toggles the bookmarks bar visibility.
  • Firefox: Press Ctrl + Shift + B (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + B (Mac) If nothing happens, enable it from the menu: View Toolbars Bookmarks Toolbar.
  • Safari: Go to View Show Favorites Bar This will show your bookmarks bar under the address bar.

Step 3: Use the Bookmarklet

  1. Open any BandLab track page you want to share. Make sure the page has loaded completely first.
  2. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar. This will copy the invite link automatically. (You may need to press it twice depending on if you have the project open or not.)
  3. Paste the link wherever you want (Discord, chat, etc.). Use Ctrl + V (Windows) or Cmd + V (Mac) to paste.
